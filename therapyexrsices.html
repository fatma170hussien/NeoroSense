Box Breathing

Breathe in for 4 seconds, hold for 4 seconds, breathe out for 4 seconds, and wait for 4 seconds. Repeat 6 times.

5-4-3-2-1 Grounding

Identify 5 things you can see, 4 things you can touch, 3 things you can hear, 2 things you can smell, and 1 thing you can taste.
