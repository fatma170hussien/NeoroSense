About NeuroSense

Where technology meets compassion to transform mental healthcare

Our Mission

We are a team who believes that mental health is a fundamental right for everyone. We created a platform that combines science, technology, and human compassion to provide a safe space that listens to you and truly understands you.

Our application uses advanced artificial intelligence that learns from the principles of real psychological therapy, communicating with you in a natural, empathetic way — like a human being who understands and cares, not just a program that responds.

Our goal is for you to find support whenever you need it, without judgment, without fear, and at any time. We are here to help you understand yourself better and regain your calm, step by step. 💙

Our Approach

We are developing a website and mobile application aimed at early detection of mental health disorders and providing simple initial support to users. The idea is based on the fact that mental health is an increasing challenge in modern societies, where many individuals suffer from conditions such as depression, anxiety disorders, post-traumatic stress disorder, personality disorders, and attention-deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD) in children. Yet, a large percentage of them do not receive early diagnosis or timely support.

The platform relies on interactive diagnostic tests, starting with a comprehensive assessment that includes multiple types of questions such as yes/no, multiple-choice, and open-ended responses, allowing the system to collect diverse data about the user. Based on the results, the user is then directed to more specialized assessments related to the potential disorder. What makes the platform unique is the integration of AI and data analysis, using machine learning algorithms and natural language processing to analyze users' written responses.

Beyond Diagnosis

The project goes beyond preliminary diagnosis; it provides the user with a simplified report that explains the results and suggests initial steps that may help, such as relaxation techniques or improving sleep habits, while emphasizing the importance of consulting a mental health professional if there are serious indicators or high-risk scores. The platform is designed to be easily accessible and affordable, making it especially useful in environments with low awareness of mental health or limited access to specialized medical care.

Innovative Technology

One of the innovative aspects of the project is the development of a smart wearable device connected to the platform, which continuously tracks physiological and behavioral indicators such as heart rate, physical activity, sleep quality, and stress levels. This data is analyzed through advanced AI algorithms to generate more accurate probability assessments of the user's mental state. Thus, the diagnosis is supported not only by interactive test responses but also by objective biometric measurements.

Additionally, the wearable can provide early alerts if indicators suggest a worsening condition and can suggest real-time breathing or relaxation exercises. This combination of wearable technology and intelligent data analysis enhances early detection accuracy and makes the project a comprehensive tool that supports not only diagnosis but also daily monitoring and ongoing treatment.

Our Impact

In this way, the project represents a scientific and social contribution by leveraging technology and AI for early detection of mental health disorders, raising awareness, reducing the gap caused by delayed diagnosis, and creating the potential for future development to include long-term condition tracking and expansion to cover additional disorders.

Our Mission

To make mental health support accessible, affordable, and stigma-free through innovative technology that understands and cares.

Our Vision

A world where everyone has the tools and support they need to thrive mentally, emotionally, and psychologically.
